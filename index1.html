About Me

Hi! I'm Prerana, a passionate traveler and storyteller from the beautiful country of Nepal 🇳🇵. I grew up surrounded by the majestic Himalayas, which sparked my love for adventure and exploration from a very young age.

I started this blog to share my travel experiences, tips, and photos with people who love to travel just as much as I do. Whether it's a weekend trip or a month-long adventure, I believe every journey teaches us something new about the world — and about ourselves.
